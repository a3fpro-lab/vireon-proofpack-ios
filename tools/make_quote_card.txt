VIREON PROOFPACK STANDARD (VPS v1.0.0)

Proofpacks are cryptographic receipts for result claims.

PASS means:
- artifacts match MANIFEST hashes (integrity)
- ATTESTATION binds manifest digest (binding)
- ≥k witnesses attest same statement (k-of-n provenance)
- transparency log anchoring checks out (log)
- replay rule passes when present (replay)

Trust-Debt Law:
Verified claims reduce “trust me” to:
“k witnesses didn’t collude + verifier spec is honest.”
